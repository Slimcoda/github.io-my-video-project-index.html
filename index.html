Our Statement of Faith

One God

We believe in one God—the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

(2 Timothy 3:16-17)

The Bible

The Bible: We believe the Bible is the inspired, trustworthy, and true Word of God, without error.

(2 Timothy 3:16-17)

Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit bears witness to the new birth and also to the entire sanctification of believers.

(2 Timothy 3:16-17)

Human Nature

Human beings are born with a fallen nature, inclined to evil continually.

(2 Timothy3:16-17)
